Information

Codenames is a game for two teams. There is a grid of 25 words. Some of them are secretly assigned to the Red Team , some to the Blue Team .
One player from each team is the Spymaster , and only Spymasters see which words belong to which team.
Spymasters take turns giving clues to their teammates (Operatives), trying to lead them to guessing their team's words. The team that guesses all their words first wins the game.

Dividing the cards

Is your team going first? You will get 9 cards the opposing team will get 8 cards. The rest of the card will be neutral and there will be 1 black card.

Card meanings

  • Red: red team guess this if your on team red for 1 point
  • Blue: blue team guess this if your on team blue for 1 point
  • Black: Assassin don't ever guess this the other team will win instantly
  • Neutral: Neutral don't guess this you won't get a point and your turn ends

Giving clues

Spymasters gives clues. When it is your turn to give a clue, say one word and a number this number is associated with the number of cards that belong to your current hint. Example hint: Metal, 2. Cards associated: horseshoe and iron After your spymaster has given the hint, he cannot speak anymore (try not to look at the spymaster since they sometimes will give facial clues)

Guessing.

(you can see the explanation of the cards again here)
If you think you know what card your spymaster is giving you a hint to click it. BUT BE CAREFUL there is no confirm option so once you click it, it will be locked in. If you guessed correctly good job you get 1 point you can either continue guessing or end your turn, has the spymaster given a hint with 2 cards? Try to find it when you are unsure it is always smart to skip your turn. If you guess a word of the opposing team your turn will end, and they will get a point (they will thank you of course). Select a neutral color? No team gets a point, and your turn will end. Picked the black card? Welp that is it the game over the other team wins regardless of points
